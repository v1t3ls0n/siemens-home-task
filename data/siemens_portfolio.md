# Siemens Digital Industries Software — Product Portfolio (public sources)

Curated from siemens.com/software and public product pages. Each "## " section
becomes one retrievable chunk in the vector store.

## Xcelerator Platform
Siemens Xcelerator is the umbrella portfolio and open digital business platform
of Siemens Digital Industries Software: software, services and an application
development platform for digital transformation. It connects design,
engineering, manufacturing and operations data across the product lifecycle,
and is the integration surface most external technologies would plug into.

## NX (CAD / Product Engineering)
NX is Siemens' flagship computer-aided design (CAD), manufacturing (CAM) and
engineering (CAE) solution for product design. Used across automotive,
aerospace, machinery and consumer products for 3D modeling, generative design,
and integrated manufacturing preparation.

## Teamcenter (PLM)
Teamcenter is Siemens' Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) system — the single
source of truth for product data: BOM management, change management,
requirements, documents, and collaboration across engineering and the supply
chain. Integrates with NX, third-party CAD, ERP (SAP), and Microsoft Teams.

## Tecnomatix (Digital Manufacturing)
Tecnomatix is the digital manufacturing portfolio: plant simulation, process
simulation, robotics simulation and offline programming, human ergonomics
simulation, and production throughput optimization. Used to design, simulate
and optimize production lines before physical build — a core "digital twin of
production" product.

## Simcenter (Simulation & Test)
Simcenter combines physics-based simulation (CFD, FEA, system simulation) with
physical testing solutions to create digital twins of product performance:
structural, thermal, flow, acoustics, electromagnetics, and system-level
modeling, plus test-data acquisition and analytics.

## Opcenter (MES / Manufacturing Operations)
Opcenter is the Manufacturing Operations Management (MOM) portfolio: MES
(manufacturing execution), advanced planning and scheduling (APS), quality
management (QMS), and manufacturing intelligence. It connects the shop floor
to the digital thread — real production data meeting planned production.

## Insights Hub (Industrial IoT, formerly MindSphere)
Insights Hub is the industrial IoT platform: connecting machines and physical
infrastructure to the cloud, collecting sensor/asset data at scale, and running
analytics and AI on operational data for condition monitoring, predictive
maintenance and energy optimization.

## Mendix (Low-Code Application Platform)
Mendix is a low-code application development platform for building web and
mobile enterprise apps quickly, including apps that extend PLM/MES/IoT data to
new users. It is the "build your own app on top of industrial data" layer of
the portfolio.

## Polarion (Application Lifecycle Management)
Polarion is the ALM solution: requirements management, test management, and
traceability for software-intensive products — increasingly important as
vehicles, machines and devices become software-defined.

## Siemens EDA (Electronic Design Automation, formerly Mentor Graphics)
Siemens EDA provides IC design, verification (Calibre, Questa), PCB design
(Xpedition, PADS) and electronic systems design tools — the electronics half
of the portfolio, from chips to boards to full electronic systems.

## Capital (Electrical / E-E Systems)
Capital covers electrical and electronic systems engineering: wire harness
design, electrical distribution systems, and network/software architecture for
vehicles and aircraft — the E/E backbone of software-defined products.

## Solid Edge (Mainstream CAD)
Solid Edge is the mainstream mechanical design portfolio for small and medium
businesses: 3D CAD, simulation, manufacturing and data management with lower
cost of entry than NX.

## Altair (Simulation, HPC & AI — acquired 2025)
Altair adds simulation and analysis (structural, electromagnetics,
multiphysics), high-performance computing orchestration, and data
analytics/AI tools to the portfolio, deepening simulation-driven design and
AI-powered engineering.
